Before we start..

there's a few things you should know!

  • 💾 Your saves will be backed up, but you should back them up too!

  • ⚠️ Patching the main game saves space, but it may cause issues updating.
  • 🪄 The mod can be automagically removed whenever you want it to be~
  • 🩷 A Mod Named Hana is fan-made and a huge work-in-progress.
